About Us

We are a team of Insurance Industry experts with a wide range of experience in claims like Fire, Marine, Engineering, Motor (Own Damage and Third Party), Liability, Credit, Personal Accident, Miscellaneous, Mediclaim etc. We are ably supported by legal experts with insurance industry experience.

Our legal experts provide the necessary guidance and representation in dealing with IRDA and Judicial and Quasi-Judicial fora like the Insurance Ombudsman, Consumer Disputes Redressal Forum, and the Civil Courts. Apart from insurance related assistance, we also carry expertise in dealing with disputes under Carriage Laws

OUR SERVICES

Claim denied by Insurance Company

We work to identify all the details which substantiate the validity of claim and thereafter take up with insurance company for speedy settlement.

Delayed Settlement

Our approach is that the surveyors and Insurance Company act in timely manner and claim gets concluded within specified timelines.

Full Claim has not been paid by the Insurance Company

We assist the insured in submitting precise documentation to the surveyors and insurance company as well as help in submitting clarifications required by insurance company so that insured gets full claim.

No Response or Clear Communication from Insurance Company

Wherein Insurance Company does not respond in a timely manner, we take up the matter with insurance company and ensure that they respond quickly.
